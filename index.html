Minesweeper made in java

.
The objetive of the game is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden mines whitout detonating any of them, with help from clues about the number of neighboring mines in each field.
Cells have three states: hidden, visible and flagged. A covered cell is blank and clickable, while an uncovered cell is exposed. Flagged cells are those marked by the player to indicate a potential mine location.
A player left-clicks a cell to uncover it. If a player uncovers a mined cell, the game ends, as there is only 1 life per game. Otherwise, the uncovered cells displays either a number, indicating the quantity of mines adjacent to it, or a blank tile, and all adjacent non-mined cells will automatically be uncovered. Right-clicking on a cell will flag it, causing a flag to appear on it. Flagged cells are still considered covered, and a player can click to uncover them, obviously they must first be unflagged with an additional right-click.
The first click in any game will never be a mine.
To win the game, players must uncover all non-mine cell.
